# SBCapstone-Project

Springboard Capstone Project

1. Decision-Support & Comparison Engine (Top Choice)

-Overview

Many people struggle with complex decisions that involve multiple competing factors, such as cost, time, risk, and long-term impact. Existing tools are often limited to spreadsheets or overly simplistic pros-and-cons lists, which do not provide meaningful structure or insight into why one option may be better than another.

-Description

This app helps users make better decisions by allowing them to define a decision, identify relevant criteria, assign weights to those criteria, and compare multiple options side-by-side. The system evaluates options based on the user’s priorities and produces ranked results with transparent scoring logic. Users can also record reflections after making a decision to review outcomes over time.

-Data Approach

The application will manage structured decision data including decisions, criteria, weights, options, and outcomes. User-generated data will be stored and analyzed to support comparisons and progress tracking.

2. Structured Reflection & Pattern-Detection Journal

-Overview

Traditional journaling tools are largely unstructured and make it difficult for users to identify patterns in their thoughts, behaviors, and emotional states over time. As a result, users often gain limited insight from their entries beyond the moment they are written.

-Description

This project is a guided journaling platform designed to help users reflect with structure and uncover recurring patterns. Users respond to prompts, tag entries with emotions, contexts, and outcomes, and review summaries that highlight trends across entries. Over time, the application helps users recognize patterns such as recurring stressors, habits, or emotional responses.

-Data Approach

The application will store journal entries, prompts, tags, and user-defined themes. Aggregated data will be used to generate summaries and pattern insights that evolve as the user continues journaling.

3. Workflow & Bottleneck Analyzer

-Overview

People often feel overwhelmed by work or daily responsibilities without understanding where their time is actually being spent or which parts of their workflow cause the most friction. Most productivity tools focus on task lists rather than analyzing inefficiencies.

-Description

This application allows users to define workflows composed of multiple steps and log time spent on each step. The system analyzes this data to identify bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and recurring slowdowns. Visual summaries help users understand where time is lost and which workflow steps may benefit from optimization.

-Data Approach

The application will collect data related to workflows, individual tasks, time logs, and performance metrics. This data will be analyzed to surface patterns and generate insights into productivity and inefficiencies.
